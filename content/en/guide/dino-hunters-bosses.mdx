<Section icon={Shield} title="Advanced Combat Mechanics">

The mechanical depth of **dino hunters bosses** becomes apparent during the "Enrage" phase. When a boss reaches a certain health threshold (usually 50% or 25%), their attack speed increases, and they may unlock new, more dangerous abilities. Mastering the dodge mechanic is the difference between a successful capture and a total wipeout.

Effective positioning is key. Most legendary dinosaurs have "blind spots"—areas behind their legs or under their tails where their primary attacks cannot reach. However, be wary of "tail swipes," which are designed specifically to counter players who stay behind the boss for too long.

| Mechanic | Boss Behavior | Player Response |
|---|---|---|
| **Ground Slam** | Boss raises front legs | Jump or dash away from the impact ring |
| **Roar Stun** | Visual soundwave effect | Use stun-resist gear or keep distance |
| **Charge** | Boss lowers head and scrapes ground | Side-dash at the last possible second |
| **Elemental Breath** | Glowing mouth animation | Move perpendicular to the boss's head |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Strategy">
Use the environment to your advantage. Many boss arenas have pillars or rock formations that can block projectiles or stop a charging dinosaur in its tracks, providing a window for free damage.
</Callout>

</Section>
