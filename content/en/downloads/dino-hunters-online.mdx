<Section icon={Map} title="Step-by-Step Beginner Progression Guide">

Starting your journey in Dino Hunters Online can be overwhelming. Follow this structured progression path to maximize your efficiency during the first few hours of gameplay.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Complete the Tutorial Quests">
    Follow the initial NPC guides to learn the basics of movement, shooting, and resource collection. This will grant you your first basic rifle and a set of hunter clothes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Farm Low-Level Zones">
    Spend time in the Starting Jungle. Focus on hunting small herbivores to gather meat and hide. Sell these resources at the central hub for Gold.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unlock Your First Mount">
    Once you reach level 10, prioritize the 'Mount Mastery' quest. Capturing a Gallimimus or a similar fast herbivore will cut your travel time by 50%.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Upgrade to Tier 2 Weapons">
    Save your Gold to purchase the Bolt-Action Rifle. This weapon allows you to safely engage medium-sized carnivores like the Dilophosaurus from a distance.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Join a Hunting Party">
    Dino Hunters Online is significantly easier with friends. Join a squad to take down your first Boss Dinosaur, which will provide the materials needed for Tier 3 gear.
  </Step>
</Steps>
