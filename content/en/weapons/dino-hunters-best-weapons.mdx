<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Step-by-Step Weapon Upgrade and Progression Guide">

Upgrading your weapons is a fundamental part of progression in Dino Hunters. As you move from temperate valley maps to harsh volcanic regions, the health pools of wild dinosaurs scale dramatically, rendering base-level weapons obsolete.

### How to Upgrade Your Arsenal

Follow these steps to systematically improve your weapons and unlock their maximum combat potential.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Gather Raw Materials">
    Explore various maps to collect scrap metal, obsidian, and rare dinosaur bones. High-tier upgrades require components dropped exclusively by legendary targets.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Access the Weapon Forge">
    Return to the main hunter hub and interact with the Weapon Forge NPC. Select your desired weapon from your inventory to view its upgrade path.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Allocate Upgrade Points">
    Prioritize upgrading raw damage and reload speed first. Magazine capacity and recoil reduction should be treated as secondary priorities.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Apply Elemental Modifications">
    Once a weapon reaches Level 10, apply elemental mods like Fire (damage over time) or Shock (crowd control) to specialize your build.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Upgrade Cost Scaling

Upgrading weapons requires a significant investment of resources. Planning your resource allocation prevents you from running out of materials before challenging major boss encounters.
