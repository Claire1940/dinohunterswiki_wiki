<Section icon={TrendingUp} title="Upgrading Your Hatched Dinosaurs">

Once you have successfully used your **dino hunters eggs** to acquire a new creature, the work has only just begun. Upgrading is the process of taking a base dinosaur and turning it into a powerhouse. You should prioritize your resources on your "Core Team"—the 3 to 5 dinosaurs you use for the majority of your hunts.

The upgrade system involves spending gold and specific monster essence gathered from successful hunts. As you increase a dinosaur's level, you will unlock new abilities and higher stat caps. Evolution is the final stage of the upgrade path, where a dinosaur transforms into a more powerful form with a completely new appearance and skill set.

<Callout type="warning" title="Resource Management">
Do not spread your resources too thin. It is better to have one level 50 Legendary dinosaur than five level 20 Common ones. Focus on your strongest hatches first.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Core Team">
    Identify the dinosaurs with the best base stats from your recent hatches. These will be the foundation of your team.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Gather Essence">
    Hunt wild dinosaurs of the same species to collect Essence. This resource is vital for breaking level caps and evolving.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Invest Gold Wisely">
    Use gold to increase levels. Costs increase exponentially, so ensure you are investing in a dinosaur you plan to keep long-term.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Evolve and Specialize">
