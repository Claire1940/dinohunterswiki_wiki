<Section icon={Target} title="Capture Mechanics & Equipment Setup">

Capturing a rare dinosaur is a multi-stage process that requires patience, precision, and the correct gear. Attempting to capture a high-tier target at full health or with basic equipment will almost certainly result in the trap failing and the dinosaur escaping or turning hostile.

The capture process is divided into four distinct phases. Following this progression path ensures the highest possible success rate while minimizing damage to your hunting gear.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate and Isolate">
    Find your target using the biome tracking methods. Clear out any surrounding smaller predators to prevent them from interfering with the capture process or damaging your traps.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Weaken the Target">
    Reduce the dinosaur's health safely. Use ranged weapons to chip away at its HP until it enters a limping or exhausted state. Avoid using high-explosive weapons that might accidentally defeat the creature.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Deploy the Trap">
    Place a high-tier trap directly in the dinosaur's path. Lead the weakened creature into the trap's trigger zone using bait or by positioning yourself as decoy.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Secure the Capture">
    Once the dinosaur is immobilized, approach it carefully and use your capture device to finalize the process. Keep an eye on the capture progress bar and be ready to deploy secondary stun nets if the target attempts to break free.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your choice of capturing equipment is just as important as your execution of the steps. Different traps offer varying levels of success depending on the size and strength of the target.

| Equipment | Success Rate | Resource Cost | Recharge Time |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Basic Net Gun** | Low | Very Low | Fast |
| **Reinforced Steel Trap** | Medium | Medium | Medium |
| **Advanced Stun Harpoon** | High | High | Slow |
| **Quantum Stasis Field** | Very High | Extremely High | Very Slow |

<Callout type="success" title="Success Factor">
Upgrading your capture device's battery capacity and reload speed in the main hub will significantly reduce the time required to secure a target, lowering the risk of third-party player interference.
</Callout>
